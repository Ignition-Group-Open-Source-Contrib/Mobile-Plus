  What is an MMS?
MMS stands for Multimedia Messaging Service and works similarly to SMS but with MMS you have the option of sending pictures, videos and tones. You must have a handset that supports this service. To activate, simply press *123# and reply with the number leading to "Device Setup."

  What is EDGE?
MTN is first in South Africa to bring you EDGE (or Enhanced Data for GSM Evolution), a 3G technology that delivers broadband data speeds to cell phones. EDGE is five times faster than conventional GPRS, is more robust, and requires an EDGE-capable cell phone. Approximately 300,000 MTN customers have EDGE-enabled cell phones.
‍
  What is GPRS+?
What's more, MTN brings you another enhancement that enables you to connect at much faster speeds even if you don't have an EDGE-enabled cellphone GPRS+! It's a faster, superior version of General Packet Radio Services('conventional' GPRS) something that your GPRS-enabled cellphone currently uses on a daily basis to transmit information. Now it all happens faster and smoother. Yet again MTN is the first in South Africa to introduce GPRS+.
